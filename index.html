Last Human Shift

A visual and sonic meditation on automation, redundancy, and the unexpected revival of human expression in an age of infinite generativity.

This image emerged from a moment of real frustration — a sense of drifting in a world where direction is increasingly dictated by black-box systems. What began as a question (“What do I even *learn* anymore to remain relevant?”) became a curiosity: How would AI *see* this confusion? What would it create, and what could it miss?

"In a future of infinite creation, will meaning itself evaporate?"
